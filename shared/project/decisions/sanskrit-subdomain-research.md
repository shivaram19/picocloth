# Sanskrit Subdomain Research for VDC Document Intelligence

**Document Curiosity Brain Research**
**Date:** 2026-04-24
**Product:** VDC (Virtual Design & Construction) Document Intelligence
**Goal:** Find evocative, short, brandable Sanskrit subdomain (e.g. `medha.trelolabs.com`) that ties architecture, intelligence, and document concepts.

## Research Sources
- Traditional Sanskrit lexicons (Vastu Shastra references)
- Wiktionary and Sanskrit dictionaries for विज्ञान (vijñāna), मेधा (medhā), etc.
- Relevance mapping to VDC: Digital architecture/construction (nirmana/shilpa), AI-driven document analysis (vijnana/medha), technical plans/specifications (sutra/prakalpa).

## Candidate Names

### 1. Vijñāna (विज्ञान)
- **Exact Meaning:** Knowledge, science, specialized wisdom, discernment.
- **Relevance to VDC:** Perfectly captures "Document Intelligence" — the scientific (vijñāna) analysis and knowledge extraction from construction documents, plans, and BIM models in Virtual Design & Construction.
- **Domain Suggestion:** `vijnana.trelolabs.com` (or `vijñana.trelolabs.com`). Check DNS with `dig vijnana.trelolabs.com`; likely available as subdomain.
- **Pros:**
  - Directly means "science/knowledge" — aligns with AI/ML document processing.
  - Modern connotation (used in Indian languages for "science").
  - Memorable and intellectual brand feel.
- **Cons:**
  - Slightly long for subdomain (7 letters).
  - Pronunciation ("vig-yaa-na") may vary for global users.

### 2. Medhā (मेधा)
- **Exact Meaning:** Intelligence, wisdom, intellect, mental vigor, retention power.
- **Relevance to VDC:** Epitomizes the "intelligence" in Document Intelligence. Medha represents the AI brain that understands, retains, and reasons over complex VDC documents (drawings, specs, RFIs).
- **Domain Suggestion:** `medha.trelolabs.com`. Short and clean; verify availability via DNS lookup or control panel.
- **Pros:**
  - Short, easy to spell/pronounce ("may-dha").
  - Strongly conveys AI "intelligence."
  - Elegant and unique.
- **Cons:**
  - Less obvious connection to "architecture/construction" without context.
  - May require branding explanation.

### 3. Sūtra (सूत्र)
- **Exact Meaning:** Thread, string, technical manual, formula, aphorism, concise rule/book.
- **Relevance to VDC:** VDC documents function like "sutras" — concise technical specifications, standards, and threaded plans that the AI intelligence weaves together for construction insight.
- **Domain Suggestion:** `sutra.trelolabs.com`. Very brandable; check for conflicts.
- **Pros:**
  - Evokes "document" or "technical blueprint" perfectly.
  - Short (5 letters), modern tech feel (like "Sutra AI").
  - Cultural depth with precision.
- **Cons:**
  - Primary meaning "thread" might confuse non-experts initially.
  - Common in yoga/Buddhist contexts.

### 4. Śilpa (शिल्प)
- **Exact Meaning:** Art, craft, architecture, technical skill, sculpture.
- **Relevance to VDC:** Refers to the architectural craft and technical design in construction. The product provides intelligence to Shilpa-related documents in the VDC workflow.
- **Domain Suggestion:** `shilpa.trelolabs.com` or `silpa.trelolabs.com`.
- **Pros:**
  - Strong tie to architecture/construction heritage (Shilpa Shastra).
  - Artistic yet technical connotation.
- **Cons:**
  - More focused on "craft" than "intelligence."
  - Pronunciation ("shil-pa") straightforward but may need transliteration guidance.

### 5. Nirmāṇa (निर्माण)
- **Exact Meaning:** Creation, building, construction, formation, manufacture.
- **Relevance to VDC:** Core to "Virtual Design & Construction" — the digital nirmana of buildings. AI analyzes documents to enable smarter construction/creation processes.
- **Domain Suggestion:** `nirmana.trelolabs.com`.
- **Pros:**
  - Directly references construction ("nirmana" is commonly used in modern Indian languages).
  - Action-oriented for a product that builds intelligence from docs.
- **Cons:**
  - Longer word; focuses more on building than document intelligence.
  - Common in business names.

### 6. Prakalpa (प्रकल्प)
- **Exact Meaning:** Project, plan, design, arrangement, proposition.
- **Relevance to VDC:** VDC revolves around project planning, design documents, and kalpa-like structured construction blueprints. The AI provides intelligence for prakalpa documents.
- **Domain Suggestion:** `prakalpa.trelolabs.com` or `kalpa.trelolabs.com` (shorter variant).
- **Pros:**
  - Strong link to "planning/design" documents.
  - Evokes structured, methodical intelligence.
- **Cons:**
  - Less known word; longer form is cumbersome.
  - "Kalpa" alone can mean "aeon" in other contexts.

## Top 3 Recommendations
1. **Medha (मेधा)** - `medha.trelolabs.com` (Top Choice)
   - **Reasoning:** Most direct match for "Document *Intelligence*." Short, elegant, easy to brand globally. Pairs perfectly with "VDC Medha" or "Trelo Medha." High memorability and conveys AI wisdom without being overly literal.

2. **Vijñāna (विज्ञान)** - `vijnana.trelolabs.com`
   - **Reasoning:** Represents the scientific/knowledge-driven approach to VDC documents. Modern, aspirational, and ties all three themes (knowledge of architectural science). Excellent for positioning as "the science of construction documents."

3. **Sūtra (सूत्र)** - `sutra.trelolabs.com`
   - **Reasoning:** Beautifully links "documents" (as technical sutras/formulas) with the intelligence that unravels them. Short, tech-sounding, and culturally rich. Ideal for a product that creates "living documents" or extracts rules from plans.

**Overall Recommendation:** Start with **medha.trelolabs.com**. It best captures the "Curiosity Brain" essence — an intelligent, wise system for VDC documents. Next steps: Check DNS availability, secure the subdomain, test branding ("Trelo Medha - VDC Document Intelligence"), and validate pronunciation with target users (architects, engineers).
