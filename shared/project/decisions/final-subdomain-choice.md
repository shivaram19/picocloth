# Final Subdomain Choice for VDC Document Intelligence

**Node-A (Curiosity Brain) Decision**
**Date:** 2026-04-24
**Product:** VDC Document Intelligence (Virtual Design & Construction)
**Domain:** trelolabs.com

## Winner: **Medha**

**Recommended Subdomain:** `medha.trelolabs.com`

**Sanskrit:** मेधा (Medhā)

**Exact Meaning:** Intellect, intelligence, wisdom, mental vigor, retention power.

## Why Medha is the Single Best Choice

After evaluating Vastu (architecture), Vijnana (science/knowledge), Prakalpa (project/plan), Nirmana (construction), and Medha (intellect) against the core needs of a **construction document AI**, **Medha** stands out as the clear winner for the following reasons:

### 1. **Perfect Semantic Fit for "Document Intelligence"**
   - The product is not just about construction (Nirmana/Vastu) or planning (Prakalpa). It is an **AI brain** that intelligently reads, understands, reasons over, summarizes, and extracts actionable insights from complex VDC documents (drawings, specifications, BIM models, RFIs, contracts).
   - "Medha" *literally means intelligence and intellect*. It positions the product as the "wise mind" or "curious brain" applied to construction documents — directly aligning with "Document Intelligence."

### 2. **Superior Brandability & User Experience**
   - Short (5 letters), easy to spell, pronounce ("MAY-dha"), and remember.
   - `medha.trelolabs.com` feels premium, modern, and tech-native (similar to successful AI brands).
   - Enables clean marketing: "Medha by Trelo Labs", "Trelo Medha — VDC Document Intelligence", or "Medha: The Intellect Behind Your Construction Documents."

### 3. **Balances All Research Themes**
   - **Intelligence/Knowledge:** Directly from the "Medha" root (stronger than Vijnana for "AI intellect").
   - **Architecture & Construction:** Implies the *intellect applied to* Vastu, Nirmana, and Prakalpa documents. The wisdom to turn raw construction docs into knowledge.
   - Avoids being too literal (e.g. Nirmana = "building" misses the AI part; Vijnana is excellent but more "science textbook" than "living intelligence").

### 4. **Strategic Advantages**
   - **Memorability & Differentiation:** Stands out in a crowded AEC/tech space. Evokes precision, wisdom, and clarity — ideal for an AI that makes sense of messy construction documentation.
   - **Scalability:** Works for future features (Medha API, Medha Insights, Medha Chat).
   - **Cultural Depth:** Sanskrit root gives authentic Indian philosophical resonance (wisdom/intellect) while remaining globally accessible.
   - **Technical Practicality:** Easy to configure as a subdomain, supports clean URLs (`app.medha.trelolabs.com`, `api.medha.trelolabs.com`), and has strong logo/branding potential.

### Comparison to Close Alternatives
- **Vijnana (विज्ञान):** Excellent runner-up (science/knowledge). More academic; "vijnana" is longer and less personal than "medha."
- **Vastu / Nirmana:** Too focused on physical building/architecture. Misses the "intelligence" essence central to the AI product.
- **Prakalpa:** Good for "planning," but too narrow and less evocative of AI wisdom.

**Medha wins decisively** because construction document AI is fundamentally an *intelligence layer* on top of VDC documents. It is the intellect that understands the plans (Prakalpa), the buildings (Nirmana/Vastu), and turns them into actionable knowledge.
