# Target Agency Battle Card — VDC Document Intelligence

## Tier 1: Boutique Specialists (Best First Customers)

### Powerkh (UK / Ukraine)
- **HQ:** London, UK / Kyiv, Ukraine
- **Size:** ~50–100 VDC specialists
- **Focus:** BIM coordination, clash detection, model authoring for UK/EU construction
- **Pain Signal:** Post-war reconstruction pipeline in Ukraine = massive document volumes, shortage of experienced staff
- **Angle:** "Scale your coordination capacity without scaling headcount. Rebuild Ukraine faster."
- **Decision Maker:** CEO or Head of VDC Operations
- **Likely Title:** "Director of BIM Services" or "Head of VDC"

### The BIM Factory (Vietnam)
- **HQ:** Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
- **Size:** ~100–200 staff
- **Focus:** Offshore BIM production and VDC services for US/AU/EU clients
- **Pain Signal:** Time-zone coordination friction, language barriers in spec interpretation, high document volume from overseas clients
- **Angle:** "Reduce misinterpretation risk on overseas projects. Your team gets instant, cited answers in English — no guessing."
- **Decision Maker:** Managing Director or Operations Lead
- **Likely Title:** "General Manager" or "Operations Director"

### BIMAGE Consulting (Singapore)
- **HQ:** Singapore
- **Size:** ~30–60 staff
- **Focus:** Digital construction, BIM management, smart city projects in Southeast Asia
- **Pain Signal:** Government smart city mandates require massive document compliance; tight deadlines; talent shortage in Singapore
- **Angle:** "Meet Singapore's digital construction mandates with AI-assisted document compliance."
- **Decision Maker:** Founder/CEO (likely smaller, founder-led)
- **Likely Title:** "Managing Director" or "Founder"
