## Outreach Templates

### Template A: LinkedIn Connection Request (Boutique Agencies)

> Hi [Name], I came across [Company] while researching VDC agencies doing innovative work in [region/market]. We're building an AI document intelligence platform specifically for VDC agencies — it auto-drafts RFIs and flags spec-drawing contradictions. Would love to show you a 10-minute demo if you're open to it. No pitch, just curious if it solves a problem you're seeing.

### Template B: Cold Email (Large Enterprise)

> Subject: Reducing spec review time at [Company]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I saw [Company] is leading VDC coordination on [project type/region]. One challenge we hear from VDC directors: senior engineers spending 30–40% of their time on document review and RFI drafting instead of high-value coordination work.
>
> We built a white-label AI platform that:
> • Answers natural-language queries across your entire document corpus with cited sources
> • Auto-drafts RFIs in your format with full document references
> • Flags contradictions between specs and drawings before they become field issues
>
> It's white-label — your clients see YOUR brand. No disruption to existing workflows.
>
> Would a 15-minute demo next week be worth your time?
>
> Best,
> [Your name]

### Template C: Follow-up (Day 7)

> Hi [Name], wanted to bump this to the top of your inbox. I know VEC teams are swamped — if the timing isn't right, totally understand. Happy to circle back in a month or send you a 2-minute Loom video instead of a live demo.

### Template D: Follow-up (Day 14)

> Hi [Name], one quick thing before I close the loop: we just published a case study showing how a UK-based VDC agency reduced spec review time by 80% on a mixed-use development project. Happy to share if it's useful, regardless of whether a demo makes sense right now.
