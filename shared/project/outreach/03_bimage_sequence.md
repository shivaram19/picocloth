## Objection Pre-emption

**"We already partner with Autodesk and other technology providers"**
> "Exactly. Autodesk provides the authoring tools. Our platform adds AI understanding on top — we don't compete with Revit or BIM 360, we make the documents those tools produce more accessible. Think of us as the intelligence layer above your existing tech stack."

**"Our clients are government and highly security-conscious"**
> "Understood. We offer dedicated tenant deployment where documents never leave your controlled environment. For highly sensitive projects, we support fully air-gapped on-premise deployment. Your security team can audit the architecture before any documents are uploaded."

**"We need Singapore-specific compliance"**
> "Our platform is jurisdiction-agnostic — it reads YOUR uploaded documents and answers from them. If your spec references Singapore Building Control Regulations, the answers will too. The AI doesn't inject external knowledge; it only retrieves from your source material."
