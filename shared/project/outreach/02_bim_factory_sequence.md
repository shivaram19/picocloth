1. **MIT Pedigree + Innovation Obsession** — Han Hoang's background at MIT and focus on "advanced design and construction technology" means he evaluates tools on technical merit, not marketing.
2. **Offshore Model** — Overseas clients send massive English document sets. Language/interpretation friction is a real risk. AI that cites exact source text reduces ambiguity.
