🪶 PicoCloth Outreach Engine

One-Time LinkedIn Session Capture

This captures your LinkedIn session cookies so the fleet bot can send connection requests on your behalf.

We never see your password. Only session cookies are captured.

Step 1: Log into LinkedIn

Open linkedin.com in this browser and log in normally.

If you have 2FA, complete it. Make sure you can see your feed.

Step 2: Capture Session

Click the button below. It will extract your session cookies and show them as JSON.

Step 3: Copy & Paste to Server

Copy the JSON below and save it on your server as:

shared/project/outreach/state/sessions/linkedin.json

Step 4: Run the Bot

Back on your server, run:

python3 shared/project/outreach/picocloth_outreach_engine.py --targets shared/project/outreach/targets.csv --limit 5
