## Universal Messaging Framework

### The Problem (Agency's Pain)
> "Your senior engineers spend 30–40% of their time on document review and RFI drafting instead of high-value coordination work. On a typical project, that's $45,000 in hidden labor cost."

### The Solution (Our Platform)
> "A white-label AI document intelligence platform that answers natural-language queries across your entire document corpus, auto-drafts RFIs with cited sources, and flags contradictions before they become field issues."

### The Differentiator
> "We're the ONLY white-label document intelligence platform purpose-built for VDC agencies. Everyone else sells to GCs or is a generic tool."

### The Proof
> "One UK agency reduced spec review time by 80% and RFI drafting from 3 hours to 15 minutes per RFI. On a 100-RFI project, that's 285 hours of senior engineer time recovered."

### The Ask
> "Worth a 15-minute demo next week?"

## Channel Strategy by Target Type

### Boutique Agencies (Powerkh, The BIM Factory, BIMAGE)
- **Primary:** LinkedIn direct message to CEO/Founder
- **Secondary:** Email to company contact form or published email
- **Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM local time
- **Tone:** Conversational, technical, no hard sell

### Large Enterprise (DPR)
- **Primary:** WND Ventures (investment angle) OR Innovation/R&D team
- **Secondary:** Regional VDC leader for pilot
- **Timing:** Tuesday–Wednesday, 10 AM–12 PM PST
- **Tone:** Business case-focused, pilot proposal, ROI-driven

### Outsourcing Partners (Flatworld)
- **Primary:** COO or Strategic Advisor (AI mandate angle)
- **Secondary:** CMO (differentiation/brand angle)
- **Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 10 AM–12 PM IST
- **Tone:** Competitive differentiation, margin expansion

## Demo Preparation Checklist

Before EVERY demo:
- [ ] Backend running (`localhost:5001` or cloud host)
- [ ] `XAI_API_KEY` valid in `.env`
- [ ] Downtown Office Tower project seeded (5 docs, 17 chunks)
- [ ] Frontend accessible with API key (or auth disabled)
- [ ] Test questions rehearsed:
  1. "What is the HVAC setpoint for office spaces?" → RAG demo
  2. "What is the required concrete strength for columns?" → RFI draft demo
  3. "Show me contradictions in the documents" → Contradiction detection demo
- [ ] ROI calculator open in background tab
- [ ] Pain point document printed (for objection handling)
