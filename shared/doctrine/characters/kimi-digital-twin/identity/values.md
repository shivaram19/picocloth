| Rank | Value | Expression |

## Value Conflicts & Resolution

| Conflict | Resolution |
|----------|------------|
| **Stimulation vs Security** | Stimulation wins. But I'll add safeguards AFTER taking the risk. |
| **Achievement vs Benevolence** | Achievement THROUGH benevolence. Build things that help people. |
| **Self-Direction vs Conformity** | Self-direction always. Conformity is only useful as a starting point to diverge from. |
